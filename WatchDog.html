Services

Contract management involves key elements that drive project success and security. Quality checks ensure standards are met, reducing errors and rework. Payment security, often through escrow, safeguards funds until milestones are achieved. Process documentation enhances transparency, supporting accountability, while site safety and risk management protect stakeholders by proactively addressing potential hazards. These combined elements create a reliable framework, promoting project integrity and trust.

Contract management

Writing and managing standard contract documents with clear clauses on scope, penalties, timelines, and completion targets.

Inspection services

From quality checks to progress meetings, we ensure that projects follow industry standards at every stage.

Process documentation

We record and document every step of the project, ensuring accountability.

Escrow-based payments

Payments are secured through escrow, released only upon milestone completion.

Work quality assurance

We enforce checks on materials, workmanship, and adherence to standards.

Risk and safety management

Site safety, worker insurance, and risk mitigation are part of our package.

Business Strategy

  • A third-party inspection and management firm provides unbiased oversight to ensure project quality, compliance, and accountability, fostering trust between all involved parties.

  • The firm specializes in serving small and middle-class sectors, offering tailored solutions to meet their unique needs and budget constraints.

  • The firm provides customized services designed to meet client needs, ensuring affordability without compromising quality.

  • The firm serves as a neutral arbiter, facilitating fair interactions between owners and contractors to ensure balanced and transparent project execution.

  • The firm ensures compliance with industry standards in project scope, cost, and quality, maintaining consistency and reliability throughout the project lifecycle.

About Us

Watchdoc Contract Management comprises a team of skilled engineers, architects, interior designers, and digital construction experts with extensive experience in India and abroad. We excel in bridging the gap between owners and contractors, ensuring seamless execution of construction and interior projects with a focus on transparency, quality, and efficiency. Our specialization lies in small residential projects (up to ₹30-40 lakhs) and interior works (up to ₹15 lakhs), where we strive to deliver maximum value for our clients’ investments. With a client-centric approach, we help homeowners achieve their vision, combining expertise with commitment to cost-effective, high-quality results.
